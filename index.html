Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp banking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. mix the flour, baking soda, and a salt in bowl
  3. Cream together the butter, sugar, and vanilla extract in a seperate bowl
  4. Add egg to the wet mixture and dry the mixture
  5. Gradually add the dry mixture and mix well
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips
  7. Scoop the dough onto a baking sheet
  8. Bake for 8-10 minutes until golden brown

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per servings
Calories 150

Carbohydrate 20g
